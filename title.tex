\section*{Abstract}
Protocol Berg v2 is a two-day conference dedicated to protocol research, decentralized infrastructure, and the experiences of core developers. Taking place on June 12â€“13, 2025, in Prenzlauer Berg, Berlin, the event features 108 sessions distributed across three stages and a dedicated workshop room.
